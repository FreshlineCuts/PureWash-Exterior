The PureWash Finish

Clean water in. Crystal-clear results out.

Good To Know

Answers before we arrive.

Why use RO/DI filtered water?

It removes minerals that cause spotting, so the rinse dries cleaner than regular tap water.

Are the brushes safe for windows?

Yes. Soft nylon brushes are made to loosen exterior grime while being gentle on glass, frames, and seals.

Do you offer pressure washing?

Yes, upon request. Window cleaning is our main service, and pressure washing can be discussed as an add-on when it fits the property.
